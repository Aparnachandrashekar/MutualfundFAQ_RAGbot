Choice Mutual Fund is the mutual fund arm of Choice AMC Private Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Choice International Limited, a leading Indian financial services conglomerate serving since 1992.
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) granted final regulatory approval to Choice AMC to commence mutual fund operations in August 2025.
Backed by the broader Choice Group’s experience in Indian financial markets, Choice Mutual Fund seeks to leverage strong research capabilities, disciplined risk management, and a structured investment process to create long-term wealth for investors.
